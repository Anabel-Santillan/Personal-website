C2MG Builders

I worked at C2MG builders for about a year after graduating from North Bennet Street School with a degree in carpentry. C2MG is a high-end custom home building company, so I was fortunate enough to work on very special and unique carpentry. A fun part of this job was that I never knew what was coming the next day. I worked all over the Eastern part of Massachusetts working on various jobs. Carpentry was an experience that taught me a lot of teamwork, attention to detail, and accountability. One of the company's signature phrases was "lead by serving", working here helped me grow a lot as a young person.
